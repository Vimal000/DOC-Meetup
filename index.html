DOC Meetup

A Online Doctor Slot Booking Sysytem

Our DOC MeetUP website offers ease and comforts to the patients while making their appointments with the doctor on the identical time. Our website moreover resolves the troubles that the patients have to face during the personal appointments. The website-Doc MeetUP acts as a patron, while the database containing the physician’s information, patient’s characteristics and appointment statistics will be maintained in over server.
